Policy for Distribution

Policy for Distribution of Applications and Games on Behalf of SCREW LTD.

As a company based in the UAE, Dubai, SCREW LTD. is committed to providing users with quality applications and games that are safe and secure.
To ensure that our distribution process is efficient and fair, we have established the following policy:

1. Application and Game Submission Process: All developers who wish to submit their applications or games to SCREW LTD. for distribution must go through a submission process. This process includes providing information about the app/game, its functionality, and its compatibility with various devices.
2. Review Process: After the submission, SCREW LTD. will conduct a review of the application/game to ensure that it meets the company's standards. This review will be based on the app/game's functionality, quality, and user experience.
3. Distribution: If an application or game is approved, it will be distributed through SCREW LTD.'s platform, and the developer will be notified. SCREW LTD. will take a 30% share of the income generated by the application/game, and the remaining 70% will be paid to the developer.
4. User Protection: SCREW LTD. is committed to protecting users from malicious or harmful applications and games. To ensure this, we will conduct regular security checks on all applications/games distributed through our platform.
5. Updates: Developers can submit updates to their applications/games at any time. SCREW LTD. will review these updates to ensure that they meet the company's standards before distributing them to users.
6. Dispute Resolution: Any disputes or disagreements between developers and SCREW LTD. regarding application/game distribution will be resolved through a formal process.
7. Policy Changes: SCREW LTD. reserves the right to change this policy at any time. If changes are made, developers will be notified in advance.

By following this policy, SCREW LTD. hopes to provide users with high-quality applications and games while ensuring that developers are fairly compensated for their work.
